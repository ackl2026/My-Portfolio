Investment Philosophy

Buying and holding for the long term (5-10 years). Good compounding businesses with wide moat or highly difficult to be replaced. Never buy out of FOMO or follow the herd mentality. To be disciplined and unemotional towards overall market sentiment as long as fundamentals of the business are intact.

Stock Selection

  • Monopolistic
  • Have Pricing Power
  • Has Operating Leverage
  • Has Organic Growth
  • Good Management

How Portfolio is Managed

Buy when good businesses selected are trading at an extreme discount or below fair value. Do not sacrifice the quality of the business for any low valuations. Sell only when we made a mistake on the initial thesis or when there is a better business with higher return with lower downside compared to the ones in holding.

Mapletree Logistics Trust (M44U)

Mapletree Logistics Trust (MLT) is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that focuses on owning and managing logistics assets across the Asia-Pacific region with total of S$13.4 billion Assets Under Management (AUM) as of June 30 2024.

Investment Thesis

  • The revenue is diversified throughout multiple regions.
  • Gross revenue & Net Property Income has been slightly stagnant due to macro headwind (I.E. Interest Rates and the Economy in China).
  • The management team has managed to divest assets at higher valuation and also actively acquire assets.
  • As of June 30 2024, debt maturity for FY24/25 is at $197M which is only 3%. It is favorable as interest rates will be coming down based on current news as of 29 July 2024.
  • 83% Debt are hedged at fixed interest rates.

Risk and Opportunities

  • (Risk) Management has highlighted that the current China Economy is not doing too well and there is oversupply of Logistics space in China, therefore rental revisions and income generated will be on the downtrend.
  • (Risk) The REIT might not do as well as compared to other REITs to consider such as Capitaland Acendas REIT.
  • (Opportunity) DPU is expected to be lower, however the current share price has dipped and is now sitting at a 6.77% dividend yield.
  • (Opportunity) As a long term investor, buying at the current level looks good as long as the management continues to focus on portfolio rejuvanation and prudent capital management.

Netlink Trust (CJLU)

NetLink NBN Trust’s extensive network forms the backbone of Singapore’s Nationwide Broadband Network (NBN), delivering ultra-high-speed internet access across mainland Singapore and its connected islands. NetLink NBN Trust and its subsidiaries (“NetLink”), designs, constructs, and maintains the passive fibre infrastructure, including ducts, manholes, fibre cables, and Central Offices, vital to the NBN’s operation.

Investment Thesis

  • Netlink's is the sole provider of singapore's broadband network thus the moat of this business is quite strong. This business is viewed similar to REIT where it pays out 90% of its cash to unit holders.
  • Overall gross revenue has been increasing, 2024 net income is lower due to one-off payments.
  • Most of the product segments are growing except for Ducts and manhole, Ancillary project (which was said its seasonal based on contract) and Co-location.
  • 86% of the company's pricing is regulated which provides a steady stream of income.
  • In 2024, the government has increased the regulated pricing for the next 5 years which means we will be looking at higher steady revenue till 2029.
  • Management has stated that their fibre optics can provide 10Gbps connection or higher which scales easily with the nationwide push to increase higher bandwith and connection speed.
  • The government has also announced that they will be spending 100million to support the enhancement of the country's broadband speed, and Netlink is positioned well to capture this opportunity.
  • Debt profile is at 20% leverage with roughly 75-80% of their debt is hegded at low interest rate.
  • The expenses are increasing year to year which could be a ratio to look at so that it doesnt eat into the overall bottom line of the company.
  • DPU has been rising yearly which is a good sign in line with Revenue.
  • At the current writing the Yield is at 5.89% with a price of $0.895 per unit.
  • Overall, it is a solid company with good management and has good moat and some tailwind for the near future.

ComfortDelGro is one of the largest land transport companies which operates in Singapore, Malaysia, UK, Australia, New Zealand, and China. ComfortDelGro’s businesses include bus, taxi, rail, car rental and leasing, automotive engineering services, inspection and testing services, driving centres, non-emergency patient transport services, insurance broking services and outdoor advertising.

Investment Thesis

  • In 2024, the Group’s revenue increased compared to 2023, primarily driven by acquisitions in the UK. The competition in Singapore’s Taxi & Private Hire market is expected to intensify with new entrants, while China Taxi revenues may be subdued due to an anticipated economic slowdown.
  • A2B and Addison Lee will fully contribute from 2025 onwards, enhancing the Group’s position in the premium P2P services for B2B customers.

  • In the UK and EU, London public bus contract renewals are expected to continue at improved margins, and the Greater Manchester public bus franchises commenced in January 2025.
  • The company is continuing investing into autonomous vehicle which may allow the company to penetrate or be the pioneer of Robotaxi market in Singapore.
  • In Malaysia, they are targeting to building EV infrastructure and network across Singapore and Malaysia till 2030.
  • They have increased their payout ratio to 80% which might affect their ability to payoff debt.
  • Debt and Expenses will increase significantly due to acquisitions, we will have to see how the Group manages margins going forward.
  • The group has mentioned that they will continue to tender for projects in UK and Stolkholm rail in 2025. Overall a good 2024 performance with a 5% dividend yield.

Investment Thesis

  • Amazon is still considered as a fast growing company due to acceleration of AWS and advertising revenue. Through the product segments, we can see that all products have been on an uptrend since 2021.
