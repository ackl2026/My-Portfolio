Investment Philosophy

Buying and holding for the long term (5-10 years). Good compounding businesses with wide moat or highly difficult to be replaced. Never buy out of FOMO or follow the herd mentality. To be disciplined and unemotional towards overall market sentiment as long as fundamentals of the business are intact.

Stock Selection

  • Monopolistic
  • Have Pricing Power
  • Has Operating Leverage
  • Has Organic Growth
  • Good Management

How Portfolio is Managed

Buy when good businesses selected are trading at an extreme discount or below fair value. Do not sacrifice the quality of the business for any low valuations. Sell only when we made a mistake on the initial thesis or when there is a better business with higher return with lower downside compared to the ones in holding.
